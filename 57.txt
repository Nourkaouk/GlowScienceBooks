Deserts are areas that have a very low rainfall each year - in some deserts it only rains once every 10 years! Many animals live in the desert including: Predators (eg. lions, cheetahs and leopards, jackals). Large and small mammals (eg. meerkats, giraffes, porcupines). Antelope (eg. eland, gemsbok, steenbok). Many species of birds (eg. falcons, eagles, buzzards, hawks). The social weavers are small weaver birds that build family nests where hundreds of weaver families can live! Many different reptiles (eg. puffadders, cobras, lizards). A great many insects also live in the desert, such as bees and butterflies and many more!
